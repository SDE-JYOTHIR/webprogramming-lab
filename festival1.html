diwali

diwali or dipavali is one of the biggest and auspicious festivals celebrated by hindus all around the globe. The festival of lights signifies peace and joy,victory of good over evil,and light over darkness everyday.it is one of the most symbolic hindhu festivals,and all the communities in the country celebrate it with much pomp.During this festival,people clean their homes,decorate every corner with lights,lamps,diyas,flowers,rangoli,and candles
